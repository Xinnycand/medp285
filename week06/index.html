BALLS

Pokeball

A pokeball is an item used in the Pokemon franchise/universe, that is used to capture and store wild pokemon so a trainer is able to make them their companion. While it is used for capturing and storage, it also allows the trainer to release the captured pokemon for battle and other forms of interaction.

8 Ball

In the game of pool, the 8-ball is the black ball numbered 8. It is considered special as it is the last ball that needs to be pocketed in order to determine who wins the game.
