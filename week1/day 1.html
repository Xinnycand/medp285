About Me

My name is Jennifer Zheng, I am in my junior year of college at Hunter College. My major is media studies with a focus in emerging media. I chose this major as a result of my desire to take more creative classes than strictly studying traditional books or subjects in STEM. Taking more creative and open minded classes will hopefully manifest a future and job that allows more freedom and creativity with what I create and work with while still making a living in society. During my free time, I enjoy relaxing and making the most of my time with indulgences like reading, music, dancing, and spending time with friends and loved ones.


Artist Statement

My work is heavily a form of self-expression. I like to express spurts of energy and inspiration through my work. This is typically done through visual arts or dance as they have been the most consistent forms of art in my life.


Being exposed to music at a young age, while also being a sheltered child, had a great impact on this as my energy would be directed towards copying what I saw musically on TV. Watching movies like Spice World and Hairspray has inspired me to take my love for music deeper as I felt drawn to dance and powerful visuals. Many years later, I still feel this admiration for art as it was an easy way for me to express myself when I found it hard to through words.


While my work is mainly a result of bursts of energy, it is also deeply influenced by ideas in society that I find fascinating and wish to explore and share. Learning from different cultures and environments around me has been a great inspiration as I continue to find new ideas to interpret and include in my work.


As I was more on the private and shy side as a child, creative expression was a more personal hobby which is why I do not typically direct my work towards a certain audience as I mainly work for my own self-indulgence and enjoyment. However, I have been slowly working towards sharing my work with others, whether that be friends and family or with my professors simply for an assignment.
